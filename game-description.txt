SIDI NUMAN’S ESCAPE — One-page Game Description

Overview
Sidi Numan’s Escape is a high-intensity narrative runner inspired by the relentless, “one-more-run” chase feel of arcade runners like Extreme Pamplona: you sprint forward through a hostile streetscape, leap hazards at the last moment, and constantly manage the pressure of a pursuer closing in. Here, that pressure is story-driven: Amina’s curse is not just backstory — it is the chase itself.

Story Inspiration (Arabian Nights)
The game adapts “The Tale of Sidi Numan” (see story.md) into playable set-pieces. In the source tale, Sidi discovers Amina’s ghoul-feasting secret in the cemetery, confronts her, and is punished by transformation. The game turns those moments into a sequence of escalating chases and branching fates:
- The cemetery discovery ignites the first flight through Baghdad.
- Amina’s curse transforms Sidi into a dog, forcing survival in a new body.
- The baker episode becomes a major branch: safety and a quiet life vs. pursuing restoration and justice.
- The final confrontation becomes interactive (not scripted) via a climactic duel.

Controls & Core Loop
- Jump: Space / Up Arrow / click / tap
- Objective: keep moving, avoid obstacles, and manage the “pursuit distance” until you reach story gates.
- Fail condition (chase stages): you are only “caught” when the pursuit distance reaches 0.

Levels, Branches, and Endings
Stage I — Flight Through Baghdad
After the cemetery sequence, Sidi flees through the city in human form. Survive long enough to earn a branching choice:
1) Race to the Sultan’s Palace (Stage II — The Sultan’s Quarter)
   - A higher-stakes run under guard pursuit.
   - Reaching the end triggers a story sequence where Sidi gains knowledge and a path to fight back.

2) Slip into the Old Quarter (Stage I — The Old Quarter)
   - A harsher alley run with its own obstacle set.
   - Designed to be survivable as long as you can keep Amina from closing the distance; the only ending is being caught at distance 0.
   - After 1500m on the HUD counter, Amina ramps up gradually to raise stakes without instantly ending the run.

Stage II — The Dog’s Journey
If Amina catches Sidi in Stage I, the curse lands: Sidi becomes a dog. The game shifts to a dog-runner moveset and different obstacle pacing. This stage includes a transition into the baker segment.

Choice — The Baker Branch
During the baker storyline, you choose:
- Stay with the baker → Ending: Baker (a reflective “quiet fate” conclusion)
- Follow the wise woman → a restorative sequence that sets up the final chase

Stage III — The Final Chase
The roles reverse: Sidi pursues Amina. When Sidi closes the distance to 0, the game enters a dramatic final duel rather than a fixed cutscene.

Final Duel: Rock–Paper–Scissors (RPS)
When the chase ends, the player (Sidi) and Amina enter an RPS showdown with cinematic presentation, sound feedback, and a short outcome sequence:
- If Sidi wins: the original victory path continues — Amina is transformed into a mule.
- If Sidi loses: Amina steals the magic water and reverses the curse — Sidi becomes the mule and Amina escapes into the night.

Powerups
Powerups appear in chase stages and provide short, punchy advantages:
- Shield: blocks a hit from an obstacle
- Speed: temporary speed boost for longer distance gains
- Magic: shifts the chase distance in your favor (pushes the pursuer back, or in the final chase closes Amina’s lead)

Difficulty
Difficulty changes the game’s “texture” more than raw speed:
- EASY: more forgiving spacing between hazards
- MEDIUM: the intended baseline pacing
- HARD: denser obstacle patterns and multi-spawn clusters that demand consistent timing

Tone & Experience
Sidi Numan’s Escape aims for a climactic Arabian Nights arcade thriller: fast movement, dramatic pursuit pressure, and story choices that feel like fate — with the final outcome decided by player action, not a single scripted ending.
